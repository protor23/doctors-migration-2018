workforce-migration:

Country - destination country

Country of origin - self-explanatory

Year - 2018, redundant column

Value* - the number of foreignly trained doctors who, in 2018, were registered or in the process of gaining registration to practise in a country other than the one in which they had obtained their medical education qualifications - this includes medical interns and residents.

NB. To replicate my data search, go to https://stats.oecd.org/Index.aspx?DataSetCode=HEALTH_WFMI#, and select var = foreign-trained-doctors-stock, year = 2018, in all OECD countries.

subregions: 
Credits for this dataset go to @Luke Duncalfe (https://github.com/lukes/ISO-3166-Countries-with-Regional-Codes), who has collected and maintained this data set.
Recent updates to this dataset were made on 8 December 2020 using information from UN Statistical data (https://unstats.un.org/unsd/methodology/m49/overview) and Wikipedia data (https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/ISO_3166-1#Officially_assigned_code_elements)

name - name of country

alpha-2 - ISO alpha-2 country code (standardized unique identifier of countries)

alpha-3 - ISO alpha-3 country code (standardized unique identifier of countries)

region, sub-region, intermediate-region - self-explanatory, various granularity of geographical region mapping (e.g., region = Africa, sub-region = Sub-Saharan Africa, intermediate-region = Middle Africa)

region-code, sub-region-code, intermediate-region-code country code - M49 numerical codes for geographic regions based on the UN Statistics classification

iso_3166-2 - ISO-3166-2 subdivision codes for the representation of countries and their subdivisions (https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/ISO_3166-2)
